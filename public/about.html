About Greemheld Social Research and Consulting

Greemheld Social Research and Consulting is dedicated to providing high-quality research and strategic advice to organizations working towards social betterment. We believe in the power of data to inform decisions and drive positive change. Our work spans across various sectors including health, education, agriculture, and governance, aiming to provide actionable insights for sustainable development.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To empower NGOs, government bodies, and private enterprises with rigorous, insightful social research and strategic consulting. We enable our partners to make evidence-based decisions that foster positive social impact, promote equity, and contribute to sustainable development within communities across Malawi and beyond.

Vision

To be the leading and most trusted partner in social research and consulting in Southern Africa, recognized for our unwavering commitment to methodological excellence, ethical practices, collaborative partnerships, and the tangible advancement of social well-being and equitable progress for all.

Our Core Values

Integrity

Upholding the highest ethical standards, ensuring transparency and honesty in all our research processes and client interactions.

Excellence

Striving for methodological rigor, innovation, and the delivery of high-quality, actionable insights in every project we undertake.

Collaboration

Working closely and respectfully with clients, communities, and stakeholders to co-create knowledge and achieve shared goals.

Impact

Focusing our efforts on research that leads to tangible, positive change, informs policy, and improves lives within society.

Our Approach

At Greemheld, we employ a participatory and mixed-methods approach to research, ensuring that our findings are not only statistically sound but also contextually relevant and rich in qualitative depth. We begin by thoroughly understanding our client's objectives, then tailor our methodologies—ranging from large-scale surveys to in-depth interviews and focus groups—to best address the research questions. Our analytical process is rigorous, and we prioritize clear, accessible reporting that translates complex data into actionable recommendations. We are committed to capacity building and often work with local partners to ensure sustainability and local ownership of research outcomes.
