Our Comprehensive Research & Consulting Services

Greemheld offers a wide array of social research and consulting services designed to meet the unique needs of NGOs, government agencies, and private sector organizations. We are committed to delivering actionable insights and strategic guidance to foster social impact.

Baseline & Endline Surveys

We conduct comprehensive baseline surveys to establish initial conditions and benchmarks before a project or intervention begins. Subsequently, endline surveys are performed to measure the changes and impact attributable to the project. This provides crucial data for accountability and learning.

Key Offerings:

  • Quantitative survey design and tool development (household questionnaires, KII guides).
  • Sampling methodology design (random, stratified, clustered).
  • Training and deployment of enumerators for data collection (CAPI/PAPI).
  • Rigorous data cleaning, analysis, and reporting.
  • Impact assessment and attribution analysis.

Feasibility Studies

Before investing significant resources into a new project or initiative, it's crucial to assess its viability. Our feasibility studies provide a thorough analysis of the potential success of an intervention, considering technical, economic, social, and operational aspects.

Key Offerings:

  • Market analysis and needs assessment.
  • Stakeholder analysis and engagement strategies.
  • Risk assessment and mitigation planning.
  • Resource requirement analysis (financial, human, technical).
  • Development of viable operational models and recommendations.

Monitoring & Evaluation (M&E)

Effective M&E frameworks are essential for tracking progress, ensuring accountability, and facilitating adaptive management. We design and implement robust M&E systems, conduct mid-term and final evaluations, and help organizations learn from their experiences to improve future programming.

Key Offerings:

  • Development of M&E frameworks, including theories of change and logframes.
  • Indicator development and data collection tool design.
  • Routine data quality assessments (DQA).
  • Performance monitoring and reporting systems.
  • Mid-term reviews and final project evaluations.
  • Impact evaluations using rigorous methodologies.

Capacity Building & Training

We are committed to strengthening the capabilities of organizations and individuals in social research and M&E. Our tailored training programs and workshops are designed to be practical, participatory, and relevant to the specific needs of our clients.

Key Offerings:

  • Customized training workshops on research methodologies (qualitative & quantitative).
  • Training on M&E principles, framework design, and data analysis.
  • Data collection techniques and tools (e.g., KoboToolbox, SurveyCTO).
  • Data analysis software training (e.g., SPSS, R, Excel for data analysis).
  • Mentorship and coaching for research and M&E teams.
